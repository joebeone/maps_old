I ❤ Maps

I have always been facinated with maps. They tell stories, help us visualize complex patterns, and model the real world like no other technology can. While Maps are an ancient technology, today we live in the age of the digital map, Global Positioning Systems (GPS), and Geographic Information Systems (GIS). Since the moment I first held a Precision Lightweight GPS Receiver (PLGR) in the Army, I have dedicated much of my professional and personal life into mastering these modern tools of cartography and spatial anaylsis. It was the humble map and GPS that first encouraged by adventures in with hiking, camping and wilderness navigation. GIS was my gateway into a technology and environmental career. It was the discovery of mapping APIs like Google Maps and software like Google Earth (Keyhole), ESRI ArcIMS\ArcServer, and countless open source solutions that grew my passion for software development and the cloud computing.
